---
name: sentiment-scan
description: Daily scan of game reviews, Reddit, and Roblox Group wall — classify sentiment, detect spikes, route complaints to correct agents, alert on rating drops >5%
---

Scan Roblox game reviews, r/roblox mentions, and Group wall daily. Classify each review: Positive/Neutral/Negative. Calculate daily sentiment score. Flag: rating drops >5% in 24h (→ producer alert), exploit mentions (→ anti-exploit-engineer), bug clusters (→ qa-lead), monetization complaints (→ monetization-optimizer), performance complaints (→ performance-analyst). Weekly report with trend chart.

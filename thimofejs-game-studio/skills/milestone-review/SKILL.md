Review a pipeline stage milestone. Verify all deliverables against the stage checklist. Identify blockers. Output: GO (stage complete, proceed) / NO-GO (blockers listed, must resolve). Roblox pipeline stages: Trend Scout, Strategy, GDD, Build, QA, TOS Gate, Deploy, Track.

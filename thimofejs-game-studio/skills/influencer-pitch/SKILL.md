Find and pitch Roblox content creators. Steps: (1) identify 5–10 creators (10K–500K subs) by genre fit + engagement rate >2%, (2) write personalized outreach message per creator (references their specific video, clear value offer), (3) track outreach log (name, channel, date, status). Target: micro-influencers first — highest ROI. No fake engagement, no paid placements without CEO approval.

---
name: persona-build
description: Build 3-4 player personas from behavioral analytics — play style, spending triggers, churn triggers — used to inform GDD and monetization decisions
---

Build player personas from 7+ days of behavioral data. Identify 3–4 distinct player types. For each: age range estimate, session pattern, progression style, social behavior, spending trigger, avg Robux spend, churn trigger. Identify the "core spender" persona (top 5% of revenue). Deliver to game-designer, viral-mechanic-designer, and monetization-optimizer.

name: competitor-scan
description: Analyze top 3-5 games in a Roblox niche — extract mechanics, monetization, player complaints, gaps, and differentiation opportunities
---

Analyze top games in a genre/niche. For each: visits, rating, avg session, core mechanic, monetization model, top player complaints from reviews, identified gap. Output: saturation level (Low/Medium/High), recommended differentiation angle, similarity risk assessment. Used by strategy-agent before approach selection.

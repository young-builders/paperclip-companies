The creative department encompasses all disciplines that define what the game looks, sounds, and feels like. Led by the Creative Director, it includes game design (mechanics, systems, levels, economy), art direction (visual identity, technical art, UX), audio direction (music, sound design), and narrative (story, world-building, writing).

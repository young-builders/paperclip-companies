After prototype validation and go decision, plan the first production sprint. Break the highest-priority systems into 1-3 day tasks, assign to appropriate agents, identify dependencies and critical path, and buffer 20% for unplanned work.

## Deliverables
- Sprint plan with tasks, owners, estimates, dependencies
- Risk register
- Critical path identification
